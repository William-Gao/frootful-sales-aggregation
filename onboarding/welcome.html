Welcome to Frootful

Thank you for installing Frootful! This extension helps you extract and analyze email content from Gmail.

Getting Started

  1. 1

    Sign in with Google

    Click the Frootful icon in your browser toolbar and sign in with your Google account.

  2. 2

    Open Gmail

    Navigate to Gmail and open an email you want to extract.

  3. 3

    Click "Extract"

    Look for the "Extract" button in the email toolbar and click it.

  4. 4

    View Extracted Content

    A sidebar will open displaying the extracted email content.

Permissions

Frootful requires access to your Gmail to function properly. We only access the emails you explicitly choose to extract, and we never store your data on our servers.
